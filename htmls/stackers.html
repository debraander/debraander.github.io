Hair Stacker

A hair stacker is an essential tool for straightening up the tips of many types of fibers.

To use a stacker, place the funnel into the stacker. Put a small clump of hair into the funnel, tips first. Tap the stacker firmly against a hard surface a few times. Remove the funnel and grasp the tips of hair fibers. The tips should then be perfectly aligned.
