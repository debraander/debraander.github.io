Mickey Finn

The Mickey Finn is a classic bucktail streamer.

  • Details
  • Materials
  • Tutorial

With the Mickey Finn, you will hand stack and then create the hair wing of a bucktail. Because a bucktail streamer has hair wings, it is more durable.

The Mickey Finn is an excellent baitfish imitation. The red stripe of bucktail resembles the lateral line of a small minnow or trout.

Swim the Mickey Finn slowly or quickly, gracefully or erratically, deep or shallow in streams or lakes for almost any game fish: trout, bass, panfish, and even some salt-water species.

  • Hook: Mustad 9672; 2-12
  • Thread: Black 3/0
  • Weight: (optional) Fine lead wire
  • Ribbing: Oval silver tinsel
  • Body: Flat silver tinsel
  • Hackle: Yellow and red bucktail, or calf tail
