Getting Started

The first step in tying a fly is setting the head of the vise at a 30 to 45 degree angle and placing the hook so the shank is horizontal.

The jaws of the vise should grasp the hook at the lower part of the bend, not at the point. If you plan to mash the barb of the hook, do it before you begin tying.
