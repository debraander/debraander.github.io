Light Cahill

The Light Cahill is a popular traditional wet-fly pattern.

  • Details
  • Materials
  • Tutorial

The Light Cahill imitates a number of major midseason mayfly species.

The fly's light coloration makes it an ideal pattern for mornings, evenings, or any low-light situations.

  • Hook: TMC 3761; 10-16
  • Thread: Cream 6/0
  • Tail: Lemon Wood Duck Flank Fibers
  • Body: Cream Rabbit Dubbing
  • Hackle: Cream Hen or Rooster Saddle
  • Wing: Lemon Wood Duck Flank Fibers
