Scissors

As you can see, there are many, many types of scissors that can be used for tying flies. The most important features are that the scissors be very sharp and have a fine point for close-in work. Stainless steel and ceramic models are the best. Ceramics cost more but never need sharpening.

Many flytiers keep their scissors in the palm of their hand while they tie. This is a good habit to learn, because you will always know where your scissors are. Spring-type scissors (see image below) are designed for palming.

Also, be sure to use scissors that are comfortable.
