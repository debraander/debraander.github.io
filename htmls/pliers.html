Hackle Pliers

Hackle pliers simplify the job of winding hackle. Select a model that will grasp the hackle tip without slipping or cutting the hackle.

To figure out how to use the hackle pliers, you really need a demonstration, so watch the video to the right to see how it's done.

By the way, in the fly tying world, hackle has multiple meanings. In this context, it refers to the neck feathers of a bird.
